                                                                                                                        FORGET

  •       Good             is a known quantity.
  •       Good             is what we all agree on.
  •       Growth          is not necessarily good.
  •       Growth          is an exploration of unlit recesses that
                                may or may not yield to our research.
                                                                                                                                    As long as you stick to good,

                                                                                                                                    you’ll never have real growth.
